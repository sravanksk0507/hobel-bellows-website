Who We Are

Engineering Precision Since Decades

Hobel Bellows Co. is one of the largest manufacturers of metallic bellows and related flexible products. With a world-class 180,000 sq ft manufacturing facility in VSEZ, Visakhapatnam, India, we combine skilled craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Our strong, debt-free financial foundation and commitment to quality have made us a trusted OEM supplier to global giants in automotive, locomotive, power generation, and general engineering sectors.

We take pride in our in-house computerized design, evaluation, and testing capabilities, ensuring every product meets the most demanding specifications.
